Thresh

The Chain Warden


He was a devote and humble warden with the job to keep save the arcane knowledge, but one day in the bless islands, the lake of life was desecrated, this caused the release of a dark magic that killed everyone including Thresh. Instantly everyone started coming back in the form of spectres and Thresh was delighted with his new abilities. Now he spends his days in the fields of Runaterra snatching the souls of his enemies.

Thresh is a semi-tank champion in the game League of Legends, which is mainly used in the supporting role. His skill set makes it ideal for starting team fights, while taking care of his most fragile teammates. Among its strengths and weaknesses we have the following:

  • Great ways to initiate fights
  • Excellent mobility on the map
  • With his passive can stack ap and armor
  • He can save teammates with his lantern
  • Good crowd control in his skills
  • High difficulty to master him
  • Long coldown in his skills
  • He is prone to be poke
  • Big punishments for mistakes
  • Needs a good ADC to shine
